The Malpractice Trap: Why Cloud AI Hallucinations Are an Uninsurable Liability in Healthcare

If this is happening in law, what will a medical malpractice jury do when a cloud AI invents a patient's allergy history? This research brief examines the case law, the mathematical solution, and why custody of your AI is custody of your liability.
