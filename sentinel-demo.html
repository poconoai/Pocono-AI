What Your Current AI Vendor Retains — In Their Own Words

Every quote below is sourced directly from the vendor's published privacy policy or data processing agreement. These are not our claims. They are the vendors' own disclosures about what they do with your data.

OpenAI (ChatGPT)
Retains conversations for up to 30 days by default for safety monitoring. Enterprise and API users can opt out of training data use but logs are retained.
Human reviewers may access conversations to improve model safety.
Under subpoena, OpenAI is required to disclose retained logs to legal authorities.
Source: OpenAI Privacy Policy & Enterprise Privacy FAQ
Microsoft Copilot / Nuance DAX
Nuance DAX is HIPAA-eligible with a signed BAA — but data still travels to Microsoft Azure for processing.
Microsoft retains diagnostic and interaction data. Azure logs are retained per your Azure subscription terms (typically 90 days).
A signed BAA governs handling after transmission — it does not prevent the data from leaving your building.
Source: Nuance HIPAA Statement & Microsoft DPA
Otter.ai
Otter.ai retains all audio recordings and transcripts. No BAA is available on free or Pro plans.
Recordings may be used to improve transcription models.
Any clinical or legal conversation transcribed through Otter.ai is retained on Otter's servers with no HIPAA protection.
Source: Otter.ai Privacy Policy
Sentinel Node (Pocono AI)
Nothing is retained on any external server. Processing is local.
No vendor has access to your queries, documents, or responses.
Under subpoena, there is no external vendor log to produce. The data does not exist outside your building.
BAA executed — but compliance is architectural, not contractual.
Verified by live tcpdump during deployment. See Trust & Security →
