What Your Current AI Vendor Retains — In Their Own Words

Every statement below is sourced from the vendor's published privacy policy or data processing agreement. Not our claims — their own disclosed terms.

OpenAI (ChatGPT)
Conversations retained for up to 30 days by default for safety monitoring.
Human reviewers may access conversations to improve model performance.
Under subpoena, OpenAI is required to disclose retained conversation logs.
Source: OpenAI Privacy Policy & Enterprise Privacy FAQ
Microsoft Copilot / Nuance DAX
Nuance DAX is HIPAA-eligible with a BAA — but data still routes to Microsoft Azure for processing.
Azure interaction data retained per subscription terms, typically 90 days.
A signed BAA governs handling after transmission. PHI still leaves your building.
Source: Microsoft HIPAA Compliance & Azure DPA
Otter.ai
Retains all audio and transcripts. No BAA available on free or Pro plans.
Recordings may be used to improve transcription models.
Clinical visits transcribed through Otter.ai are retained on Otter's servers — no HIPAA protection.
Source: Otter.ai Privacy Policy
Sentinel Node (Pocono AI)
Nothing retained externally. All processing is local.
No vendor accesses your queries, documents, or responses.
Under subpoena: no external log exists to produce.
BAA executed — compliance is architectural, not contractual.
Verified by live tcpdump at deployment. See Trust & Security →
