Enterprise Service Level Agreement

Effective Date: April 2026

Covered Infrastructure: Sentinel Node™ On-Premises Hardware & Local Software Stack

1. Zero-Access & Data Custody Declaration

Pocono AI, LLC ("Provider") deploys physically isolated, air-gapped hardware ("Sentinel Node"). At no time does the Provider require, request, or possess remote access to the Sentinel Node or the network on which it resides. The Client retains absolute custody of all data, Protected Health Information (PHI), and legally privileged information processed by the Sentinel Node.

HIPAA/HITECH Status: Because the Provider cannot access, transmit, or store the Client’s data, the Provider is strictly a hardware/software licensor and operates outside the scope of a Business Associate under HIPAA.

2. Service Level and Response Times

Support is provided for hardware failures and local software pipeline anomalies. Because the Sentinel Node does not rely on cloud connectivity, traditional "Cloud Uptime" metrics do not apply. Support requests are categorized by the Client upon submission to the Secure Portal:

Severity 1 (Critical) 2 Hour Response

Definition: Complete hardware failure or localized inference engine crash halting all processing.

Resolution Action: Remote diagnostic guidance or immediate dispatch of replacement hardware (See Section 3).

Severity 2 (High) 4 Hour Response

Definition: RAG pipeline degradation (e.g., specific document ingestion failure, noticeable latency).

Resolution Action: Provision of a cryptographic software patch or localized database rebuild script.

Severity 3 (Standard) 24 Hour Response

Definition: Feature inquiries, update requests, or general maintenance questions.

3. Hardware Replacement Guarantee

For hardware leased or maintained under an active Pocono AI support tier:

  • Standard Tier: Next Business Day (NBD) shipment of replacement components (e.g., eGPU, processing node) via overnight courier.
  • Enterprise Redundancy Tier: Client is provided a cold-standby "Hot Spare" Sentinel Node on-site. In the event of a Severity 1 failure, Client IT swaps the physical NVMe storage to the Hot Spare for immediate return to operation within 15 minutes.

4. Software, Weights, and Security Updates

To maintain the Enterprise Autonomy Architecture (EAA) and air-gapped security, updates to the Large Language Models (LLMs) or retrieval architecture are never pushed via the internet.

  • Updates are delivered exclusively via cryptographically signed executable packages available in the Secure Portal.
  • It is the sole responsibility of the Client’s IT administration to download, verify the checksum, and manually apply the update to the Sentinel Node via physical media.

5. "Human-in-the-Loop" Mandate & Limitation of Liability

The Sentinel Node is strictly an assistive administrative and discovery tool.

  • Mandatory Verification: The AI system will cite the localized data chunk used to generate any summary or output. A licensed professional (e.g., Physician, Attorney) MUST review and verify all outputs against the cited source before committing the data to an official Electronic Health Record (EHR) or legal filing.
  • No Professional Advice: Outputs generated by the Sentinel Node do not constitute legal or medical advice.

Liability: Pocono AI, LLC is not liable for medical malpractice, legal malpractice, misdiagnosis, or erroneous filings resulting from the Client’s failure to verify AI-generated outputs. Maximum liability under this SLA shall not exceed the amount paid for the Sentinel Node deployment in the preceding 12 months.
