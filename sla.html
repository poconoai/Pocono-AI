Enterprise Service Level Agreement

Effective Date: April 2026   |   Last Reviewed: April 2026
Covered Infrastructure: Sentinel Node™ On-Premises Hardware & Local Software Stack
Parties: Pocono AI, LLC (“Provider”) and the deploying practice or organization (“Client”)

1. Zero-Access & Data Custody Declaration

Pocono AI, LLC (“Provider”) deploys physically isolated, air-gapped hardware (“Sentinel Node”). At no time does the Provider require, request, or possess remote access to the Sentinel Node or the network on which it resides. The Client retains absolute custody of all data, Protected Health Information (PHI), and legally privileged information processed by the Sentinel Node.

HIPAA/HITECH Status: Because the Provider cannot access, transmit, or store the Client’s data during standard inference operations, the Provider operates as a hardware and software licensor outside the scope of a Business Associate under HIPAA. A fully executed Business Associate Agreement (BAA) is provided before any authorized on-site maintenance visit that may involve incidental access to the Client’s environment.

2. Service Level & Response Times

Support is provided for hardware failures and local software pipeline anomalies. Because the Sentinel Node does not rely on cloud connectivity, traditional “Cloud Uptime” SLA metrics do not apply. Response time commitments are contingent on the Client maintaining an active, paid support tier. Support requests are categorized by the Client upon submission to the Secure Portal:

Severity 1 — Critical ● 2-Hour Response

Definition: Complete hardware failure or localized inference engine crash halting all processing for all users.

Resolution: Remote diagnostic guidance initiated immediately. For Enterprise Redundancy Tier clients, the on-site Hot Spare node absorbs the workload — Client IT swaps NVMe arrays targeting restoration in under 15 minutes. For Standard Tier, Dell ProSupport Plus hardware dispatch is initiated under the underlying manufacturer warranty, coordinated by Pocono AI on the Client's behalf. See Section 3 for full hardware replacement terms.

Severity 2 — High ● 4-Hour Response

Definition: RAG pipeline degradation — specific document ingestion failures, noticeable retrieval latency increase, or partial user access loss.

Resolution: Provision of a cryptographically signed software patch or localized vector database rebuild script delivered via Secure Portal.

Severity 3 — Standard ● 24-Hour Response

Definition: Feature inquiries, model update requests, configuration questions, or general maintenance questions not affecting primary system availability.

Resolution: Written response via email or Secure Portal ticket.

Support Hours & Tier Dependency: Response time commitments apply during standard business hours (Mon–Fri, 9 AM–6 PM Eastern) for Standard and Clinical SLA subscribers. Sovereign tier subscribers receive priority coverage including weekend Severity 1 response. Response commitments are suspended if the Client’s active SLA subscription has lapsed.

3. Hardware Replacement Guarantee

The following guarantees apply to hardware deployed under an active Pocono AI SLA subscription:

  • Standard Tier: Next Business Day (NBD) shipment of replacement components (e.g., eGPU, processing node, NVMe storage array) via overnight courier at no additional charge for manufacturing defects within the warranty period.
  • Enterprise Redundancy Tier: Client is provided a cold-standby “Hot Spare” Sentinel Node on-site at deployment. In the event of a Severity 1 failure, Client IT swaps the physical NVMe storage arrays to the Hot Spare for immediate return to full operation, targeting restoration within 15 minutes of swap initiation.

Keep Your Hard Drive (KYHD) — Mandatory PHI Custody Provision

All Sentinel Node deployments operating under an active SLA are provisioned with the Keep Your Hard Drive (KYHD) add-on under their Dell ProSupport Plus coverage. This is not optional for any deployment involving Protected Health Information (PHI), privileged legal documents, or other regulated data.

Under standard Dell ProSupport contracts, a technician dispatched for hardware failure is required to return the failed drive to Dell. Under KYHD, the Client retains full physical custody of the failed drive. The defective component remains in the Client’s possession — to be destroyed, degaussed, or archived per the Client’s own data retention policy — and a replacement drive is provided without requiring return of the original.

This provision closes the final gap in the PHI custody chain. The Sentinel Node ingests locally. Processes locally. And if hardware fails, your data never leaves your building — not even on a defective drive headed to a third-party repair facility.

Hardware replacement does not cover physical damage caused by the Client, acts of third parties, or environmental conditions outside standard operating specifications. Underlying manufacturer warranties (Dell ProSupport Plus with KYHD) apply and are facilitated by the Provider on the Client’s behalf.

Software Restoration Protocol — Post Hardware Replacement

Dell ProSupport Plus replaces physical hardware. It does not reinstall the operating system, vector database, or AI model weights. After any Severity 1 hardware event requiring Dell component replacement, the following Pocono AI software restoration sequence applies:

  1. Hardware Triage: Client IT and Dell ProSupport coordinate physical component replacement (motherboard, NVMe array, or processing node). KYHD ensures all data-bearing drives remain in Client custody throughout.
  2. Restoration USB Dispatch: Pocono AI dispatches a cryptographically signed restoration USB within 4 business hours of confirmed hardware replacement. The USB contains the complete OS image, Qdrant vector database configuration, and model weight loader.
  3. Client IT Installation: Client’s IT administrator boots from the restoration USB and applies the signed image. Estimated installation time: 45–90 minutes. Step-by-step written runbook provided.
  4. Vector Database Rehydration: The document ingestion pipeline re-indexes from the Client’s local document archive. Pocono AI provides a rehydration script; no data leaves the building at any stage.
  5. Functional Verification: Chief Technologist confirms system is operational via phone walkthrough with Client IT before closing the ticket.

Target full software restoration: under 24 hours from confirmed hardware replacement for Standard Tier. Enterprise Redundancy Tier clients remain operational on the Hot Spare during the entire process.

4. Software, Model Weights & Security Updates

To maintain the Enterprise Autonomy Architecture (EAA™) and air-gapped security posture, updates to Large Language Models (LLMs), retrieval architecture, or system software are never pushed via the internet.

  • Updates are delivered exclusively via cryptographically signed executable packages made available through the Secure Portal.
  • Every update package includes a SHA-256 checksum. The Client’s IT administrator must verify this checksum against the value published in the Secure Portal before installation.
  • It is the sole responsibility of the Client’s IT administration to download, verify, and manually apply updates to the Sentinel Node via physical media (USB or equivalent).
  • Pocono AI, LLC is not responsible for system vulnerabilities or model drift resulting from the Client’s failure to apply published updates in a timely manner.

5. Human-in-the-Loop Mandate & Audit Log Specification

Mandatory Clinical & Legal Requirement: The Sentinel Node is strictly an assistive administrative and discovery tool. Outputs generated by the system do not constitute medical advice, clinical diagnosis, legal advice, or legal strategy. All outputs must be verified by a licensed professional before use in any official capacity.

Mandatory Verification Protocol: The Sentinel Node cites the specific local document chunk used to generate every summary or output. A licensed professional (Physician, Attorney, or delegated qualified staff member) MUST review and verify all AI-generated outputs against the cited source chunk before committing any output to an official Electronic Health Record (EHR), clinical note, legal filing, or patient care decision.

No Professional Advice: Pocono AI, LLC provides infrastructure. The Provider does not practice medicine, provide clinical guidance, provide legal counsel, or render professional opinions of any kind through the Sentinel Node system.

Audit Log Architecture: To enforce and support the Human-in-the-Loop requirement, every query session is logged locally on the Sentinel Node hardware and is never transmitted to Pocono AI, LLC or any external party.

Each Audit Log Entry Captures:
  • The verbatim user prompt submitted to the system
  • The specific local document chunks retrieved by the RAG pipeline, with source file name and page reference
  • The complete model-generated response
  • A UTC timestamp and local session identifier
  • The quantized model version and parameter count active at time of query
  • The local user account identifier associated with the session

Minimum audit log retention is 90 days on local NVMe storage. The Client is solely responsible for configuring extended retention periods to meet applicable regulatory requirements (HIPAA minimum: 6 years for BAA-related records). Audit logs are accessible to authorized Client administrators via the local interface at any time and are never accessible to the Provider.

6. Limitation of Liability

Pocono AI, LLC is not liable for medical malpractice, legal malpractice, misdiagnosis, erroneous patient care decisions, or erroneous legal filings resulting from the Client’s use of, or failure to verify, AI-generated outputs against the cited source data chunks provided by the system.

Maximum Liability Cap: In no event shall Pocono AI, LLC’s total aggregate liability to the Client under this SLA — for any cause whatsoever and regardless of the form of action — exceed the total amount paid by the Client to Pocono AI, LLC for the Sentinel Node deployment and active SLA subscription in the twelve (12) months immediately preceding the claim.

Exclusions: Pocono AI, LLC shall not be liable for: (a) indirect, incidental, consequential, special, or punitive damages of any kind; (b) loss of revenue, profits, or business opportunities; (c) data loss resulting from the Client’s failure to maintain independent local backups; or (d) damages resulting from the Client’s failure to apply available security updates.

The Client explicitly acknowledges that the Sentinel Node is a workflow augmentation tool operating under a mandatory Human-in-the-Loop protocol, and that all final professional judgments — clinical, legal, and administrative — remain the sole responsibility of the licensed professional operating the system.

7. Force Majeure

Neither party shall be held liable for delays or failures in performance resulting from causes beyond their reasonable control, including but not limited to: acts of God, natural disasters, power grid failures, government actions, supply chain disruptions affecting hardware component availability, or other events that make performance commercially impracticable. The affected party shall notify the other party in writing as soon as practicable and shall resume performance as quickly as reasonably possible.

8. Dispute Resolution

In the event of a dispute arising under this SLA, the parties agree to first attempt good-faith resolution through direct negotiation between authorized representatives, with a minimum 30-day negotiation period before either party may initiate formal proceedings.

If negotiation fails, the parties agree to non-binding mediation administered by a mutually agreed mediator in Pennsylvania before resorting to litigation. The cost of mediation shall be shared equally between the parties.

9. Governing Law & Jurisdiction

This SLA is governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania, without regard to its conflict of law principles. Any legal action brought solely under this SLA — and not under a separately executed Deployment & Services Agreement — shall be brought in the state or federal courts located within the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania.

Clients requiring their home-state jurisdiction or federal arbitration clauses may negotiate alternative governing law terms in the separately executed Deployment & Services Agreement signed at time of procurement.

10. Amendments & Modifications

Pocono AI, LLC reserves the right to update this SLA with reasonable written notice of not less than 30 days delivered to the Client’s designated contact on file. Continued use of the Sentinel Node system following the notice period constitutes acceptance of the updated terms. Material changes to response time commitments, liability caps, or hardware replacement terms require written acknowledgment from the Client before taking effect.

No amendment, modification, or waiver of any provision of this SLA shall be valid unless made in writing and signed by an authorized representative of both parties.
