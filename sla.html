Service Level Agreement

Effective Date: April 2026  |  Last Updated: April 2026  |  Questions: marcus@poconoai.com

Note: This is a summary SLA for reference. Deployed clients receive a full executed Deployment & Services Agreement specifying their individual SLA tier, hardware configuration, and support escalation path. Contact marcus@poconoai.com for your deployment-specific agreement.

1. Hardware Coverage — KYHD (Know Your Hardware Down)

All Sentinel Node deployments include the manufacturer's enterprise hardware SLA. For Dell-based configurations, this is Dell ProSupport Plus with 4-Hour Mission Critical On-Site Response, available 24×7×365, including holidays.

  • Response time: On-site technician dispatched within 4 hours of hardware failure confirmation
  • Parts: Critical components covered including CPUs, RAM, storage arrays, and GPU cards
  • Coverage: 3-year coverage included in all HaaS subscriptions; extended terms available

2. Software & Configuration Support

Pocono AI, LLC provides direct engineering support for the Sentinel Node's software stack — model configuration, RAG pipeline, vector database, and system updates.

  • Standard response: Next business day for non-critical issues
  • Priority response: 4-hour response for system-down conditions during business hours (M–F, 9am–6pm ET)
  • Model updates: Delivered via cryptographically signed USB media, scheduled quarterly or as needed for security patches
  • Remote diagnosis: Performed via secure, client-initiated screen share only — no persistent remote access

3. HaaS Subscription — What's Included

The Hardware-as-a-Service (HaaS) monthly subscription covers all of the following at a single predictable rate:

  • Sentinel Node hardware (Pocono AI, LLC retains title; client has exclusive on-premises custody)
  • Operating system and firmware maintenance
  • LLM model updates and RAG pipeline upgrades
  • Qdrant vector database maintenance
  • Manufacturer hardware SLA (KYHD)
  • Human-in-the-Loop protocol documentation and training materials
  • 90-day audit log architecture (on-device, client-controlled)

4. Exclusions

This SLA does not cover:

  • Damage resulting from client network misconfiguration, power surges, or physical damage outside manufacturer defect
  • HIPAA compliance attestation (client remains the Covered Entity — see Terms of Service)
  • Client data backup, disaster recovery planning, or business continuity services beyond hardware replacement
  • EHR integration, API development, or third-party software support

5. Uptime & Availability

The Sentinel Node operates on the client's local network without cloud dependencies. System availability is therefore a function of the client's local power and network infrastructure, not Pocono AI, LLC's infrastructure. Pocono AI, LLC commits to hardware that meets or exceeds Dell enterprise specifications for mean time between failures (MTBF).

6. SLA Disputes

SLA disputes are governed by the terms of the executed Deployment & Services Agreement. For clients without a signed agreement, Pennsylvania law governs. Contact marcus@poconoai.com to initiate any SLA dispute resolution process.
