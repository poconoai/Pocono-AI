The Zero-Integration Deployment Model

Most AI vendors require FHIR API integration, IT approvals, and months of implementation. The Sentinel Node bypasses all of that by operating on standard PDFs — adjacent to your EHR, not inside it.

01

Export from EHR

Physician or MA exports the relevant chart history as a standard PDF to a local secure workstation. No PHI leaves the local network.

02

Sentinel Node Processes Locally

PDF is transferred to the Node — a physical appliance in your office. It ingests, vectorizes, and generates a summary using local models. Zero internet connection needed.

03

Cited Output Delivered

The Node generates a clinically or legally relevant summary. Every claim is cited to a specific page number in the source document. The professional verifies citations before acting.

04

Human Reviews & Acts

The physician or attorney reviews the summary, verifies citations, and manually enters relevant content into their EHR or case file. The Node logs the full inference chain for auditability.
