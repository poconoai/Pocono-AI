Client Portal

Secure access for active Pocono AI deployment partners and pilot program participants.

Access Under Provisioning Portal authentication is currently in active development. Pilot program participants receive credentials directly from the Chief Technologist. Contact marcus@poconoai.com or call (570) 534-0602.

⚠ Portal Scope & Air-Gap Integrity Notice This Client Portal is strictly limited to billing management, asynchronous support ticketing, and documentation access. It maintains zero network connectivity, zero API access, and zero telemetry hooks into your local Sentinel Node hardware. The portal operates on an entirely separate, isolated web infrastructure. Your Sentinel Node remains physically and logically air-gapped at all times — this portal cannot initiate, receive, or relay any communication with your deployed hardware under any circumstance. If you have questions about network isolation architecture, contact marcus@poconoai.com.
