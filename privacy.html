Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 2026

1. Infrastructure vs. Web Perimeter

Pocono AI, LLC maintains a strict separation between our public marketing perimeter (poconoai.com) and our product deployments (The Sentinel Node™). This policy governs the public website.

No BAA Initiation: Interaction with this website, including submitting inquiries via contact forms or email, does not constitute or initiate a Business Associate Agreement (BAA) under HIPAA. Deployed Sentinel Nodes are 100% locally hosted, air-gapped, and do not transmit telemetry, PHI, or EPHI to Pocono AI, LLC or any third-party cloud provider.

2. Data Collection on PoconoAI.com

We believe in minimal data collection. We utilize privacy-first analytics (Umami) which does not use cookies, does not track users across sites, and fully complies with GDPR and CCPA. We collect basic, anonymized metrics (e.g., page views, referring domains) to maintain site performance.

3. Contact Forms and Communications

When you submit an inquiry via our public form or email, we collect the professional contact information provided (Name, Email, Message). This data is used strictly for B2B communication regarding deployment logistics. We explicitly forbid the submission of Protected Health Information (PHI) via our public web forms.

4. Zero Third-Party Selling

We do not rent, sell, or share professional contact data with third-party marketers or data brokers. Our business model is infrastructure deployment, not data monetization.

5. Inquiries and Compliance

For questions regarding our privacy practices, Enterprise Autonomy Architecture (EAA™), or to review our compliance framework, please contact the Chief Technologist at: marcus@poconoai.com.
