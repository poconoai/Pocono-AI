Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 2026  |  Last Updated: April 2026  |  Questions: marcus@poconoai.com

1. Who We Are

Pocono AI, LLC is an on-premises AI infrastructure company headquartered in East Stroudsburg, Pennsylvania. We build and deploy the Sentinel Node™ — a physically isolated, zero-exfiltration AI system for independent medical practices and law firms. We do not operate cloud AI services.

2. This Website

This policy covers the poconoai.com marketing website only. The Sentinel Node hardware itself operates entirely offline — it collects no data from patients or clients, transmits nothing to Pocono AI, LLC, and has no outbound telemetry of any kind.

3. What We Collect on This Website

We collect the minimum information necessary to operate a professional website:

  • Contact form submissions: If you submit a contact or scheduling request, we collect your name, email address, and message. This information is used solely to respond to your inquiry.
  • Analytics: This site uses Umami, a privacy-first, open-source analytics tool. Umami collects anonymized page view data with no cookies, no cross-site tracking, and no personal identifiers. We use this data only to understand general site traffic patterns.
  • Server logs: Standard web server logs (IP address, browser type, pages visited, timestamps) are retained for security and operational purposes for up to 30 days.

We do not use third-party advertising pixels, retargeting scripts, Facebook Pixel, Google Analytics, or any cross-site tracking tools.

4. What We Do Not Collect

  • Patient health information (PHI) or protected health information of any kind
  • Client legal information or privileged communications
  • Payment card data (processed by third-party payment processors under their own privacy policies)
  • Biometric data, precise geolocation, or behavioral tracking data

5. How We Use Your Information

Contact information is used exclusively to respond to your inquiry and, if you opt in, to send relevant product updates. We do not sell, rent, or share your contact information with third parties for marketing purposes.

6. Data Retention

Contact inquiries are retained for the duration of our business relationship and up to 3 years thereafter. Server logs are purged after 30 days. Anonymized analytics data is retained indefinitely as aggregate statistics with no personal identifiers.

7. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal information we hold about you by contacting marcus@poconoai.com. Pennsylvania residents have additional rights under applicable state law. We will respond to all requests within 30 days.

8. Security

We apply industry-standard security practices to protect information submitted through this website. Given the nature of our business — building air-gapped AI infrastructure — data security is a core operational value, not an afterthought.

9. Changes to This Policy

We will post any material changes to this policy on this page with an updated effective date. We will not retroactively reduce your rights without direct notice.

10. Contact
