What Actually Stops a Breach

The cloud AI industry's answer to breach risk is a Business Associate Agreement — a contract that promises careful handling of your data after it has already left your building. A BAA does not prevent a breach. It determines liability after one happens.

Cloud AI Approach

Sign a BAA. Your PHI travels over the internet to a remote server. The vendor promises to handle it carefully. When a breach occurs, the BAA determines who pays. The breach still happened.

Sentinel Node Approach

No transmission. PHI is processed locally on hardware in your building. There is no journey over the internet. There is no remote server. A BAA is still executed — but the compliance case is architectural, not contractual. The breach cannot happen remotely.
