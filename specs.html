Platform & Physical Specifications

Base Platform

Dell Precision 7960 Tower Workstation (or equivalent enterprise chassis)
Dual independent Hardware RAID controllers for OS/Data isolation
TPM 2.0 module, chassis intrusion detection, optional Kensington lock
Dell ProSupport Plus 4-Hour Onsite SLA included

Power & Environment

Power: 300W typical, 750W maximum (110-240V auto-switching)
Dimensions: 17.3" H x 6.8" W x 17.6" D (439mm x 173mm x 447mm)
Weight: 38-45 lbs depending on configuration
Acoustics: <35 dB idle (clinic-quiet operation)
Operating temp: 10°C to 35°C, 20-80% non-condensing humidity
