Common Questions

Do I own the hardware?

Yes. Hardware is purchased separately and is yours from day one. The monthly HaaS fee covers the software, model weights, SLA support, and ongoing configuration — not the hardware. You retain 100% physical custody.

What happens if I cancel?

You keep your hardware. The local model weights already on your machine continue to function as configured at time of cancellation. You simply lose access to future model updates, SLA support, and configuration changes.

Is the monthly fee tax-deductible?

Monthly HaaS fees are structured as operating expenses, not capital expenditures — 100% deductible in the year incurred (no depreciation schedule). Consult your tax advisor for your specific situation.

What does the implementation timeline look like?

From signed POV agreement to first productive inference: 2–4 weeks for most solo practices. This includes hardware procurement, VLAN configuration, RAG pipeline setup, and HITL workflow calibration with your team.

Do you work with our MSP?

Yes. We've built an MSP Partner Program specifically for this. Your existing IT provider can be involved at the referral or installation level. See the Partners page for program details.

What models run on the Sentinel Node?

Current deployment stack: Ollama as the inference engine, running Gemma and Llama 3/4 variants. Model selection is tuned at deployment for your use case. All models run fully locally — no API calls to any cloud provider.
