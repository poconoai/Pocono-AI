Enterprise Sales & Revenue Operations Lead

💼 Sales / Business Development 📍 East Stroudsburg, PA (Hybrid/Remote) 🤝 B2B Enterprise

The Role

Independent medical and legal practices are desperate for AI that reclaims their time without leaking sensitive data to the cloud. The Sentinel Node™ is built, priced, and ready. We need a closer who speaks both the language of physicians and the language of procurement — and who wants to be an owner, not just a rep.

What You Will Own

  • Pipeline Development: Identify and qualify independent clinics and law firms (2–10 providers) for Paid Proof-of-Value engagements.
  • Technical Demos: Run live walkthroughs of the Sentinel Node alongside our engineering team — no cold decks, real hardware.
  • Deal Closure: Shepherd prospects from first call through architecture assessment to signed SLA.
  • Partner Outreach: Develop referral relationships with EHR implementation consultants, healthcare attorneys, MSPs, and practice managers.

Who You Are

  • Proven B2B closer — you have sold hardware, software, or services into regulated industries (healthcare or legal preferred).
  • Comfortable talking to a CISO about air-gap architecture and a practice manager about time reclamation in the same week.
  • You want a foundational role with meaningful equity upside, not just a quota.

Compensation

Structure: Base + commission on closed deployments, with equity (ownership shares in Pocono AI, LLC) offered to early team members in lieu of above-market cash compensation. Not a capital investment requirement — sweat equity only.

Stage: Ground-floor — compensation scales with the first funded round, currently in progress.

Junior DBA — Field Deployment Specialist (Multiple Openings)

🗄️ Database / IT Operations 📍 East Stroudsburg, PA — Field Travel: NJ · NYC · Philly 🚗 Client-Site Travel Required

The Role

We are building a team of junior DBAs who thrive being in the field — hands on hardware, on-site at client locations, solving real problems in real environments. Sentinel Node™ deployments live inside the walls of medical practices and law firms in the Pennsylvania Poconos, throughout New Jersey, greater Philadelphia, and the New York City metro area. You will be the person who makes that deployment sing on-location.

This is not a remote-only, ticket-queue role. We need people who want to drive, show up, and own the outcome. We are hiring multiple people for this role as we scale across the Mid-Atlantic. If you have DBA fundamentals, a valid driver's license, and genuine curiosity for how AI infrastructure fits into secure regulated environments — we want to hear from you.

What You Will Own

  • On-Site Deployment: Travel to client locations to physically rack, configure, and validate Sentinel Node™ hardware and software stack.
  • Database Ops: Qdrant vector database initialization, local SQL schema setup, and indexing pipeline configuration tailored to each client's document environment.
  • Client Handoff & Training: Walk clients through their system post-deployment — making sure staff are confident before you leave the site.
  • Troubleshooting: Field calls and site visits for post-deployment issues; own first-line resolution, escalate to senior engineering as needed.
  • MSP Coordination: Many clients have existing MSP relationships. You will coordinate directly with their IT partners on network integration and support handoffs. Our MSP Partner Program is central to how we scale — you'll work alongside those partners in the field.

Who You Are

  • Some hands-on DBA or IT experience — formal degree not required, demonstrated competence required.
  • Comfortable with Linux environments, basic SQL, and command-line tooling.
  • Valid driver's license and willingness to travel across PA, NJ, and NY regularly.
  • You like being on-site solving real problems — not just tickets.
  • You communicate clearly and professionally in client-facing situations. You represent the brand on the ground.

Compensation

Structure: Hourly or salary commensurate with experience, with equity (ownership shares in Pocono AI, LLC) available to early team members. Mileage reimbursement for field travel.

Stage: Multiple seats available now and expanding with funding milestone.
