HIPAA Enforcement Is Accelerating

OCR penalties for PHI breaches reached record levels in 2024. Shadow AI use by clinical and administrative staff is now the fastest-growing breach vector — and regulators have begun explicitly referencing AI tool usage in enforcement actions. Independent practices are the least-defended target in the ecosystem.

Epic, Nuance, and Azure OpenAI are built for hospital systems with dedicated IT staff and six-figure implementation budgets.
