The Market Opportunity

Physicians spend an average of 5.9 hours daily in the EHR — nearly 44% of their working day on clerical tasks rather than patient care. The U.S. has over 1 million active physicians. The legal sector faces an identical burden in discovery and document review.

Cloud AI solves the efficiency problem but creates an existential compliance problem: HIPAA prohibits sending PHI to OpenAI, Google, or Microsoft. Every cloud AI vendor is architecturally incompatible with the healthcare market's core legal requirement.

This is not a niche problem. It is a structural gap that no funded competitor currently fills.

The Pocono AI Solution

The Sentinel Node™ is a fully localized, air-gapped AI hardware appliance installed inside the client's facility. All vectorization, inference, and logging occur on hardware the client physically owns. Zero data leaves the building. Zero cloud telemetry. Zero HIPAA exposure.

This is not a software-only play. The hardware custody model creates a defensible moat: competitors cannot replicate the zero-exfiltration guarantee without abandoning their cloud architecture entirely.

The Business Associate Agreement (BAA) is structured around the EAA™ deployment model — language no cloud vendor can offer their clients.
