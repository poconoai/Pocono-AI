Sovereign Architecture

Local Hardware. Global Intelligence. Zero Cloud Risk.

The Data Sovereignty Pipeline

Phase 01

Local Ingestion

The Sentinel Node pulls data directly from your local EHR or legal database via secure read-only bridges. Data remains strictly within your LAN.

Phase 02

Vectorization & RAG

Information is converted into mathematical vectors and stored in a local, encrypted vector database. No data is routed to OpenAI or external embedding APIs.

Phase 03

Private Inference

Our custom-tuned LLMs process your queries on high-performance NVIDIA hardware inside the node. The "thinking" happens inches away from your data, governed by strict retrieval verification protocols and transparent audit logs.

Air-Gapped Maintenance

Our Outbound-Only Update Protocol ensures the system stays current without creating a backdoor. System health and model weights are updated via cryptographically signed packets. We never pull PHI or sensitive case files back to our servers. Ever.
