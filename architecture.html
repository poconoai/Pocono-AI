Sovereign Architecture

Local Hardware. Global Intelligence. Zero Cloud Risk.

The Data Sovereignty Pipeline

Phase 01

Local Ingestion

The Sentinel Node pulls data directly from your local EHR or legal database via secure HL7/FHIR or SQL bridges. Data remains within your LAN.

Phase 02

Vectorization & RAG

Information is converted into mathematical vectors and stored in a local, encrypted vector database. No data is sent to OpenAI or cloud-based embedding services.

Phase 03

Private Inference

Our custom-tuned LLMs process your queries on high-performance NVIDIA hardware inside the node. The "thinking" happens inches away from your data.

Air-Gapped Maintenance

Our Outbound-Only Update Protocol ensures the system stays current without creating a backdoor. System health and model weights are updated via cryptographically signed packets. We never pull PHI or sensitive case files back to our servers. Ever.
