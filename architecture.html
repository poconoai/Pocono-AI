Sentinel Node · Technical Architecture

Sovereign by Design

Every component of the Sentinel Node runs inside your building, on your hardware, under your control. No cloud APIs. No data egress. No exceptions.

Zero Cloud Exposure HIPAA-Native LAN-Only Inference Open-Weight Models Audit-Traceable RAG
01 · Data Sovereignty Pipeline

How Your Data Moves — And Where It Stays

Three isolated phases, all on-premises. At no point does your data cross a network boundary outside your LAN.

Phase 01

Local Ingestion

Documents, EHR exports, case files, and structured records are ingested directly from your local network via read-only bridges. Nothing touches the public internet.

LAN-only bridge PDF · DOCX · HL7 Read-only access
Phase 02

Local Vectorization & RAG Index

Text is chunked and converted into embeddings by a locally-running model. Vectors are stored in an encrypted on-disk vector database — no external embedding API calls.

Qdrant (local) nomic-embed-text AES-256 at rest
Phase 03

Private Inference

Queries retrieve relevant document chunks from the local index, which are passed to an open-weight LLM running entirely on-device. Every response is source-cited and logged.

Ollama runtime Llama 3 / Mistral Immutable audit log

03 · Update & Maintenance Protocol

Outbound-Only Update Protocol

The Sentinel Node never receives unsolicited inbound connections. Model weight updates and system patches are delivered as cryptographically signed packages initiated from within your network — not pulled from our servers. Your PHI, case files, and document index are never transmitted outbound. Ever. Maintenance can also be performed fully offline via physical media on request.

05 · Compliance & Auditability

Built for High-Liability Environments

Every response is source-cited. Every query is logged. Nothing is a black box.

  • Source-cited responses — every AI answer references the exact document and page it was drawn from
  • Immutable query audit log — who asked what, when, and what source documents were retrieved
  • HIPAA-native architecture — no PHI leaves your network at any layer of the stack
  • BAA available — Business Associate Agreement provided prior to deployment
  • Attorney-client privilege preserved — case documents never transmitted to third-party AI infrastructure
  • No hallucinated citations — RAG retrieval grounds all answers in your actual documents
  • EHR-agnostic ingestion — works alongside Epic, AthenaHealth, and major practice management systems
  • Offline-capable — full functionality with zero internet connection if required
