Computing Sovereignty

Our architecture is built on the principle that sensitive data should never leave the physical control of the professional. The Sentinel Node™ is a localized "Fortress" for your data, combining high-speed retrieval with air-gapped security protocols, ensuring absolute compliance with HIPAA, HITECH, and maintaining strict Attorney-Client Privilege.

Production Environment: The Sentinel Node

Our deployed solutions are built on rock-solid, industry-leading architecture designed for 24/7 reliability and massive computational workloads. We do not rely on the cloud; we bring the cloud to you.

Dell Precision Chassis

Standardized on Dell Precision 5000 & 7000 Series workstations for enterprise-grade thermal management and continuous uptime.

Tiered System Memory

Configurable RAM scaling: 64 GB RAM for standard deployment, or 128 GB RAM for massive parallel document retrieval and intensive RAG frameworks.

Enterprise NVIDIA GPUs

Bypassing consumer cards for professional A-Series architecture (A4500, A5000, A6000), designed specifically for AI inference, deep learning, and flawless stability.

Redundant Solid State Storage (RAID 1)

The operating system, local LLMs, and vector databases are mirrored across high-speed SSDs. This guarantees zero data loss and frictionless hardware upgrades—when it is time to scale up, we simply migrate your encrypted drives to the new Sentinel Node chassis for immediate deployment.

Research & Development: The Proof of Concept Lab

Innovation requires pushing hardware to its absolute breaking point. Before any software update or Large Language Model makes its way to a Sentinel Node, it must first survive our internal Proof of Concept (PoC) testing environment.

Resource-Constrained Testing

We utilize an optimized Dell Latitude mobile workstation equipped with 32 GB of RAM to guarantee our local LLMs and vector math run flawlessly on lean hardware.

Legacy GPU Baseline

Tested via high-bandwidth eGPU configurations housing a legacy NVIDIA GTX 1080 Ti. Forcing extreme efficiency here ensures blistering performance on A-Series production chips.

Air-Gapped Logic

Hardware-level isolation ensures zero telemetry across both R&D and production. No data is ever sent to external servers, preserving HIPAA and legal privilege.
