Sentinel Node · Technical Architecture

Sovereign by Design

Every component of the Sentinel Node runs inside your building, on your hardware, under your control. No cloud APIs. No data egress. No exceptions.

Zero Cloud Exposure HIPAA-Native LAN-Only Inference Open-Weight Models Audit-Traceable RAG
01 · Data Sovereignty Pipeline

The On-Premises Flow

Three isolated phases. At no point does your data cross a network boundary outside your LAN.

Phase 01

Local Ingestion

Documents, EHR exports, and case files are ingested directly via read-only bridges.

LAN-only Read-only
Phase 02

Local Vectorization

Text is chunked and stored in an encrypted local database. No external APIs used.
