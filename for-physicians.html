Administrative burnout is not a workflow problem. It's a patient safety problem.

When you spend 44% of your day on documentation, something else suffers. Research links physician burnout directly to diagnostic error, reduced empathy, and patient communication breakdown. The paperwork isn't just stealing your time — it's stealing your capacity to care.

The Sentinel Node doesn't replace clinical judgment. It removes the clerical wall between you and your patients — so you can show up fully, without the keyboard competing for your attention.
