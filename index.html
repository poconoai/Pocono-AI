Technical Brief: Achieving Zero Telemetry

The conversation around AI safety often misses a fundamental structural reality: If you want to stop an AI model from "phoning home," you can't just ask it nicely not to. You have to engineer an environment where it physically cannot.

At Pocono AI, we enforce strict hardware and network firewalls. A proper sovereign deployment places the AI infrastructure on a physically isolated network—such as a dedicated 10.x.x.x subnet—routing traffic in to cordon the system off from outbound access.
