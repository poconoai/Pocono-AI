The Solution

Hours back Measuring in Active Pilots

The Sentinel Node™ automates chart prep, document summarization, and clinical triage locally. Early workflow modeling targets 3–5 hours of daily reclamation — we are measuring actual time savings with our first pilot partners and will publish the results.

Human-in-the-Loop protocol required: Licensed professionals verify all cited local data outputs. Time savings vary by practice volume and workflow.

Operational Deployment Model

Reduced Exfiltration

Prevents PHI exfiltration by obviating outbound cloud connections for primary inference workflows.

Auditable Local AI
