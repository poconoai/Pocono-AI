Operational Deployment: How It Connects

The Sentinel Node is designed for immediate operational utility without compromising your existing network security. We deploy in two distinct phases to manage risk and integration costs.

1

Hardware Delivery

The physical Sentinel Node (Dell Precision array) is delivered and installed directly behind your facility's firewall. It operates on your local area network (LAN) with all outbound telemetry physically or logically severed.

2

Phase I: Isolated Terminal

Immediate Zero-Risk Utility. Staff utilize our secure, browser-based local interface to manually ingest specific clinical notes, legal briefs, or patient histories. This "air-gapped clipboard" approach requires zero EHR API integration, ensuring absolute control over what the model sees from Day One.

3

Phase II: Legacy Systems Bridging

Automated Pipeline. Once the local RAG utility is proven via isolated terminal mode, our architecture allows for the future build-out of custom, read-only API bridges directly into your legacy EHR or practice management software.
