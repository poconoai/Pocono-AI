Technical Brief: Achieving Zero Telemetry

The conversation around AI safety often misses a structural reality: If you want to stop an AI model from "phoning home," you have to engineer an environment where it physically cannot.

At Pocono AI, we enforce strict hardware and network firewalls. A proper sovereign deployment places the AI infrastructure on a physically isolated network—routing traffic in to cordon the system off from outbound access.
