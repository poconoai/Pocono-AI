Academic medical centers and specialized clinics face a compounding administrative burden: specialists spend upwards of two hours post-clinic parsing dense referral packets, prior notes, and lab histories in Electronic Health Record (EHR) systems like Epic, Oracle Cerner, Athenahealth, and eClinicalWorks. While cloud-based LLMs offer summarization, they are fundamentally incompatible with strict data sovereignty and hospital compliance requirements.

Pocono AI offers an immediate, isolated solution. The Sentinel Node™ provides localized, hardware-accelerated Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) designed to reclaim valuable clinical hours without relying on your EHR interface team or exposing Protected Health Information (PHI) to the cloud.

The Zero-Integration Workflow

We do not require FHIR API integration or app marketplace approvals to begin delivering value. The pilot operates entirely adjacent to your existing systems:

  1. EHR Export (No PHI Leaves the Local Network): The physician or Medical Assistant exports the required chart history (consult notes, labs, referral documents) directly from the EHR (e.g., Epic MyChart, Cerner) as a standard PDF to a local, secure workstation.
  2. Local Processing via Sentinel Node: The PDF is transferred to the Sentinel Node—a dedicated hardware appliance located physically in the clinic. The Node runs a localized AI model to read, parse, and summarize the documents.
  3. Instant Synthesis with Citations: Within seconds, the Node generates a concise, clinically relevant summary. Crucially, every claim is cited directly to the source page numbers of the original PDF, ensuring total physician oversight and dramatically mitigating the risk of AI "hallucinations."
  4. Manual EHR Entry: The physician reviews the localized summary, verifies the citations, and manually copies the required text into their encounter note.

Why IT, Legal & Compliance Teams Approve This Pilot

  • Isolated Security Architecture: The Sentinel Node does not require outbound internet access. It can operate as an entirely offline appliance or as a restricted endpoint on an isolated clinic VLAN.
  • Zero Cloud Exfiltration: No data is ever sent to OpenAI, Google, or any external API. All inferencing and vector storage happens strictly on the local hardware.
  • Operates Independently of EHR Interfaces: By operating on standard PDFs, we bypass the 12-18 month queue typical for HL7/FHIR integration approvals and deep IT resource allocation.
  • BAA Ready: Pocono AI executes a standard Business Associate Agreement (BAA) for the pilot, treating the physical appliance as a secured, localized endpoint under your existing compliance framework.
The Proof is in the Packets: We offer a 30-minute technical walkthrough for your IT and security teams, including a live network packet capture ("tcpdump") diagnostic to demonstrate zero network exfiltration during model inference.

The 60-Day Paid Proof-of-Value Deployment
