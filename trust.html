The Pillars of Sovereignty

The Privacy Paradox

The operational bottleneck where organizations require AI efficiency but cannot risk the HIPAA violations or loss of privilege inherent in third-party cloud processing. The Sentinel Node resolves this paradox by removing the cloud entirely.

Cloud Obviation

Not cloud avoidance — cloud elimination. The Sentinel Node processes all inference, retrieval, and storage locally. There is no API call to make. No transmission to intercept. No remote server to breach. Your compliance case is architectural.

Transparent Auditability

Every query, every retrieved chunk, every generated output is logged locally. Citations trace directly to page numbers in source documents. Your IT team or compliance officer can audit the full inference chain — no vendor access required.

Air-Gapped Option

The Sentinel Node can operate on a physically isolated VLAN with no outbound internet access whatsoever. Updates are delivered via cryptographically signed offline packets. This is not a setting — it is a physical architecture choice you make at deployment.

Why Not Just Use ChatGPT?

Cloud AI requires your data to leave your building. Ours doesn't.

Every cloud AI tool — no matter what their privacy policy says — requires your patient records or client files to travel over the internet to a remote server to be processed. That transmission is the risk. A Business Associate Agreement promises careful handling after your data has already left your building.

The Sentinel Node makes that journey impossible. It processes everything locally. There is no transmission to promise. There is no server to breach. Your compliance case is architectural — not contractual.

Compliance Architecture

BAA Ready

HIPAA by Architecture

HIPAA compliance through the Sentinel Node is not contractual — it is physical. PHI cannot be exfiltrated because the system is designed to prevent outbound data transmission during inference. This is architecturally categorically different from a signed agreement.

Attorney-Client Privilege

For legal practices, cloud AI introduces a genuine privilege waiver risk when confidential client communications and case documents are transmitted to third-party servers. The Sentinel Node eliminates the transmission entirely.

No FHIR/EHR Integration Required

The zero-integration workflow operates entirely adjacent to your existing EHR. No HL7/FHIR API approvals, no IT queue, no 12-18 month wait. PHI stays local as a PDF export — processed on the Node, never transmitted.

Legal Precedent

Cloud AI Hallucinations Are an Uninsurable Liability

In Mata v. Avianca and the Raine v. OpenAI case, attorneys faced sanctions and a $110,000 federal penalty for AI-fabricated citations submitted to courts. Cloud AI models generate from parametric memory — they can confidently cite sources that do not exist.

The Sentinel Node's local RAG forces every output to cite a retrieved document chunk from your actual files, traceable to a specific page number. The physician or attorney verifies the citation before acting. This is the Human-in-the-Loop (HITL) architecture — not a disclaimer, but a cognitive handoff point.
