Prior Authorization & Denial Defense

Winning the Prior Authorization Fight With Complete Local Patient Evidence

13 hours per week. $68,000 per physician per year. The equivalent of 100,000 full-time registered nurses consumed by paperwork. Prior authorization is the largest documented source of administrative waste in American medicine — and the practices that win are the ones that produce complete, cited, patient-specific evidence fastest.

The Problem

A nurse building a prior authorization submission currently hunts across multiple EHR tabs, scanned documents, and payer portals to assemble the patient history that supports the request. 82% of appealed denials are ultimately overturned — meaning most denials are wrong on the merits — but only 0.2% of patients ever appeal, because practices lack the time and staff to fight every denial. (Sources: AMA 2024 Annual PA Survey; Health Affairs / Stanford, January 2026.)

The Sentinel Node™ Workflow

BeforeHunt across 4–6 EHR tabs and payer portals. 20–40 minutes per request. Incomplete documentation increases denial rate.
With SentinelSingle local query: "Patient's full history with Drug X, prior adverse events, and past payer approvals." Cited answer in seconds. Complete documentation. Submission ready.

How data gets to the Node: During the 60-Day POV, we configure a local secure drop-folder on your existing network. Staff export from the EHR once — the Sentinel Node indexes automatically. From that point, queries run from any authorized workstation on your LAN. No manual file transfers per query. No new logins. The workflow is functionally invisible after setup.

Reclaimed: Staff time reduced from 20–30 minutes to 2–3 minutes per authorization. Complete patient-specific evidence reduces initial denial rate and strengthens every appeal.
